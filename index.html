About Me

My name is Jeff. I'm a 31 year old manager at El Encanto Restaurants, and I'm working hard to learn web and software development via University of Arizona's Coding Bootcamp. So far I've found coding in html and css to be a lot of fun, and look forward to learning javascript and more server side languages. My goal is to be able to make a change in careers in the next year. Outside of work and education, my hobbies include hiking, riding my motorcycle, and homebrewing. I'm a huge craft beer fan, and have even brewed on a couple of large systems at local breweries. I have a wife, Kady, who is a picu nurse at Phoenix Children's Hospital, and 2 dogs, Auggie, and Scooter; all of whom I love very much. Anyways, please consider me for your next Oscar nomination and thank you for your time!
